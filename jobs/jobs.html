Senior Full Stack Engineer (Remote)

Tech Innovators Corp

Remote - Full-time

Easy Apply - Actively recruiting

About the Role

Tech Innovators Corp is seeking a highly skilled and motivated Senior Full Stack Engineer to join our fully remote team. You will be instrumental in designing, building, and maintaining our next-generation platform features, working across the entire stack from database to user interface.

We are looking for someone who thrives in a fast-paced environment and is passionate about delivering high-quality, scalable code.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and deploy robust backend services using Node.js and REST APIs.
  • Build responsive and high-performance user interfaces using React, Tailwind CSS, and modern state management.
  • Collaborate with product managers and designers to translate requirements into technical specifications.
  • Mentor junior engineers and promote best practices for code quality and testing.

Minimum Qualifications

  • 5+ years of professional experience in software development.
  • Expertise in JavaScript/TypeScript, Node.js, and a modern frontend framework (React, Angular, Vue).
  • Strong proficiency with SQL/NoSQL databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
